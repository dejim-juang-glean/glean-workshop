(Optional) Module 3b: Post-Workshop Activities!

You have seen an overview of how Glean Search and Assistant works, but there are quite a few additional features of Glean! Please feel free to utilize the sandbox environment to explore and test some relevant uses for your team! Below are a few suggested features to look through and learn about!

Glean can be accessed from app.glean.com (via our webapp) or through several different avenues. Our goal is to work where you work - not change your workflow!

Glean Extension: The Glean browser extension enhances the user experience by allowing users to search across all their tools directly from their browser. It supports multiple browsers and provides quick access to Glean's powerful search capabilities, making it easier to find information without disrupting workflow.

Desktop App: Glean's desktop app offers a spotlight-like search experience, enabling users to search across all company tools with just a few keystrokes. This app is particularly useful for Mac OS users who need quick and efficient access to information.

Mobile App: The Glean mobile app ensures that users can access Glean's search capabilities on the go, providing flexibility and convenience for those who need to find information while away from their desks.

Native Search Replacement and Widgets: This feature integrates Glean's search capabilities directly into native applications like Jira, Confluence, and Drive, enhancing the search experience within these tools and allowing users to find relevant information more efficiently.

Slack Gleanbot (not available in sandbox): The Gleanbot for Slack allows users to interact with Glean directly within Slack channels. It can automatically answer common questions and provide quick access to documents, improving productivity and reducing the need to switch between applications.

Leveraging our core enterprise search technology, we are able to power AI in several advanced applications. See the features below for a better understanding of how we do so!

Apps: Glean Apps enable users to create custom generative AI assistants tailored to specific business needs without any coding. These apps can be published in the Glean App Library for easy discovery and integration into communication tools like Slack.

Actions and Developer Tools: Glean Actions allow users to perform tasks such as writing emails or filing tickets directly from the Glean interface. This feature leverages generative AI to streamline workflows and improve efficiency.

Prompts: Prompts guide users through the process of interacting with AI applications, helping them ask better questions and perform tasks more effectively. This feature enhances the user experience by making AI interactions more intuitive and productive.

Besides connecting and finding knowledge, we also want to make knowledge more accessible and organized!

People profiles and org chart: This feature provides detailed profiles and organizational charts, helping users quickly identify and connect with relevant internal contacts. It improves collaboration and communication within the organization.

Go-links: Go-links create memorable shortcuts to frequently accessed documents and resources, streamlining navigation and improving productivity by reducing the time spent searching for information.

Answer Cards and Boards: Answer Cards and Boards organize and display answers to common questions, making it easy for users to find and share knowledge ...across the organization. This feature helps in reducing repetitive queries and ensures that information is readily available to those who need it.

Collections: Collections allow users to group related documents and resources, facilitating better organization and easier access to important information. This feature is particularly useful for project management and team collaboration.

Announcements: Announcements enable organizations to broadcast important updates and information to all employees, ensuring everyone stays informed and aligned. This feature is crucial for maintaining effective communication within the company.
